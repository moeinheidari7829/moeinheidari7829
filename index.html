About Me

ðŸ‘‹ Hi! I'm a PhD student in Biomedical Engineering at the University of British Columbia, passionate about developing AI solutions that make a real-world impact.

My research sits at the exciting intersection of Computer Vision, Medical Imaging, and Deep Learning. I'm particularly interested in Implicit Neural Representations, Vision Transformers, Diffusion Models, and their applications in healthcare.

Currently, I'm working on "AI for Point-of-Care Ultrasound: Addressing Data, Deployment, and Domain Shift Challenges" at UBC's UltrAi Lab under Dr. Ilker Hacihaliloglu.

I've been fortunate to collaborate with leading researchers from Stanford, RWTH Aachen, MILA, and MBZUAI, publishing at top venues including CVPR, ICCV, MICCAI, and Medical Image Analysis.
